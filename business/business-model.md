# PySchedule Business Model Canvas

## Overview

PySchedule follows a hybrid business model transitioning from **Open-Source + Consulting** to **Education + SaaS**, leveraging the research toolkit as both a lead generation engine and product foundation.

---

## Customer Segments

### Primary Segments (Year 1-2)
1. **Cloud Infrastructure Companies** (B2B)
   - Size: 100-5000 employees
   - Value: Cost optimization ($100K-500K annual savings potential)
   - Willingness to pay: $30K-80K for implementation

2. **Manufacturing SMBs** (B2B)
   - Size: 50-500 employees
   - Value: Production efficiency (15-30% capacity gains)
   - Willingness to pay: $20K-50K for consulting

3. **Computer Science Educators** (B2B/B2C)
   - Size: Individual professors, departments, online educators
   - Value: Ready-to-teach curriculum with interactive tools
   - Willingness to pay: $299-999 per course

### Secondary Segments (Year 2+)
4. **Healthcare Systems** - Operating room/equipment scheduling
5. **Algorithm Researchers** - Free users, citations build credibility

---

## Value Propositions

### For Cloud Infrastructure Companies
**"Reduce infrastructure costs by 20-40% through optimized resource scheduling"**
- Proven DPE algorithm with academic validation
- Transparent simulation reveals hidden inefficiencies
- Rapid pilot implementation (4-6 weeks vs 6+ months)
- Measurable ROI with before/after metrics

### For Manufacturing SMBs
**"Maximize production capacity while meeting deadlines"**
- Easy-to-understand Gantt visualizations
- "What-if" scenario planning
- No complex ERP integration required
- Start small, scale gradually

### For Educators
**"Publication-quality scheduling education toolkit"**
- 24 ready-to-use scenarios with student exercises
- Beautiful visualizations for lectures/assignments
- Comprehensive documentation and API reference
- Open-source flexibility for customization

### For Researchers
**"Skip 6 months of infrastructure development, start research immediately"**
- Clean, extensible architecture
- Baseline algorithms for comparison (SPT, EDF, DPE)
- Reproducible experiments with deterministic simulation
- Academic credibility through proper citations

## Business Model Evolution

### Phase 1: Consulting-Led (Months 0-12)
- **Focus**: Prove ROI, build case studies
- **Revenue**: 100% consulting
- **Goal**: $150K revenue, 5 reference customers

### Phase 2: Hybrid (Months 12-24)
- **Focus**: Scale education, pilot SaaS
- **Revenue**: 60% consulting, 30% education, 10% SaaS
- **Goal**: $500K revenue, SaaS product-market fit

### Phase 3: Product-Led (Months 24-36)
- **Focus**: SaaS growth, consulting for enterprise only
- **Revenue**: 40% SaaS, 40% education, 20% consulting
- **Goal**: $2M revenue, VC fundable or bootstrapped profitability
